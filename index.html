Work

The CloudMagic Mail app is our take on modern email. We believe that modern email should be delightful, context aware and contemporary. Since it’s launch in the fall of 2013, we’ve quickly got it in the hands of more than a million people. CloudMagic is top rated on both iOS and Android, has been written about by WSJ and Time, and has won the Internet's highest honor - The Webbys.

You would be a key member of the team that makes all of this happen.
